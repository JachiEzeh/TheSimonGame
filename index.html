Welcome to the Simon Game

Press "Play" to Start

Simon game is an electronic game of short-term memory skill with a series of tones and lights, you are to repeat the sequence displayed by the bot. If you succeed, the series becomes progressively longer and more complex. For the first level you are to memorize the color and repeat it. From the second level and until the game ends the bot will only display one color each level. Your mission is to click on all the colors in the progression displayed by the bot before that level, before clicking on the new color displayed by the bot in the current level in order to progress to the next level.
