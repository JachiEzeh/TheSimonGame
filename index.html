Welcome to the Simon Game

Press "Play" to Start

Simon game is an electronic game of short-term memory skill with a series of tones and lights, you are to repeat the sequence displayed by the bot. If you succeed, the series becomes progressively longer and more complex. The first round you are to memorize the color and repeat it, from the second rounds and until the game ends the bot will only display a color you are to play all the colors in the progression displayed by the bot before that round before adding the new color the displayed by the bot in the current round to progress to the next round.
